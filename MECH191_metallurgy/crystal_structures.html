Face-Centered Cubic (FCC)

In FCC structure, atoms are located at each of the eight corners and at the center of each face. This arrangement is also known as cubic close-packed (CCP). The coordination number is 12, meaning each atom touches 12 neighbors.

Coordination Number12
Atomic Packing Factor0.74 (74%)
ExamplesAl, Cu, Ni, Au, Ag, Pt
