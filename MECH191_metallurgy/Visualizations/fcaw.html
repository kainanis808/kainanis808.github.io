Slag InclusionIncomplete slag removal between passes; undercut traps slagChip and wire-brush every pass; correct undercut first

Bead Quality Indicators

Good Weld Indicators

  • Smooth, uniform ripple pattern along bead
  • Slag lifts cleanly in one or few pieces
  • Consistent bead width (within ±1 wire diameter)
  • No visible porosity after slag removal
  • Proper toe blending — no undercut groove

Problem Indicators

  • Pitted slag surface → porosity likely beneath
  • Slag bonded to bead toes → undercut or cold lap
  • Irregular ripple → arc instability or wrong polarity
  • Wide, flat bead with excessive spatter → voltage too high
  • Narrow, high-crowned bead → voltage too low or WFS too high
