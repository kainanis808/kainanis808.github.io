MECH191 Visualizations

Welcome to the MECH191 Visualizations page! Here you will find interactive visualizations and resources related to the MECH191: Metallurgy course. These visualizations are designed to help students better understand key concepts in metallurgy, such as crystal structures, phase diagrams, mechanical properties, and heat treatment processes.

Feel free to explore the visualizations and use them as a supplementary resource for your studies in MECH191. If you have any questions or suggestions for additional visualizations, please don't hesitate to reach out to the instructor.
